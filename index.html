• SUNDAR PICHAI

    Pichai Sundararajan, better known as Sundar Pichai, is an Indian-American business executive. He is the chief executive officer of Alphabet Inc.

  • ELON MUSK

    Elon Reeve Musk FRS is a business magnate, industrial designer, and engineer. He is the founder, CEO, CTO, and chief designer of SpaceX.

  • SATYA NADELLA

    Satya Narayana Nadella is an Indian-American business executive. He is the chief executive officer of Microsoft, succeeding Steve Ballmer in 2014.

  • MARK ZUCKERBERG

    Mark Elliot Zuckerberg is an American media magnate, internet entrepreneur, and philanthropist. He is known for co-founding Facebook, Inc.

  • JEFF BEZOS

    Jeffrey Preston Bezos is an American internet entrepreneur. Bezos is the founder and CEO of the multi-national technology company Amazon.

  • TIM COOK

    Timothy Donald Cook is an American business executive who has been the chief executive officer of Apple Inc. since 2011.
